About Me

Aleksandra Elcsics

Aspiring Website Developer and UI/UX designer

Hello! My name is Aleksandra Elcsics and I am a Computer Science and Marketing major at the University of Connecticut! I am a sophomore that has a passion in coding and website development. I love to problem solve and create something that many different people can use. I use my ideas to be creative and innovative, finding new ways to spread new purposes!

In addition to this, I have used countless software's for projects and designs that were for classwork, social media, or just personal use. With this being said, I am open to learning new applications to get more well-rounded in other areas. On top of that, I have used AI and computer vision in some projects to connect with future technology. I plan to learn upon these topics even more to continue to strengthen my knowledge.

Lastly, I am part of social media marketing within the University of Connecticut's Taekwondo Club. Here I have the role of Social Media Chair, where I create posts and designs for the team. This includes Instagram posts to designing merchandise. I combine marketing and efficiently advertising to get my messages across.
